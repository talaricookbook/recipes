- [ ] 2 cups all-purpose flour (250g)
- [ ] 3 tbsp white sugar (38g)
- [ ] 1.5 tsp baking soda
- [ ] 1.5 tsp baking powder
- [ ] 2 tsp kosher salt
- [ ] 2.5 cups buttermilk (560g)
- [ ] 2 large eggs
- [ ] 3 tbsp unsalted butter, melted (43g)
- [ ] 1 tsp vanilla extract

## ðŸ¥˜ Instructions
### 1. Mix Ingredients
- In a bowl, whisk together dry ingredients.
- Add in buttermilk, eggs, butter (cooled), and vanilla extract
- Whisk until dry mixed

### 2. Cook
- Grease a pan / skillet with butter
- Add pancake mix to hot pan (medium-low heat)
- Cook until bubbles start to slow down, then flip
- Continue cooking until the pancake rises, then starts to fall slightly
- Remove from pan and tent under foil until served 

### 3. Serve
- With warmed maple syrup

- Don't overmix, lumps are ok
- Leftovers are good for 5 days in the fridge
