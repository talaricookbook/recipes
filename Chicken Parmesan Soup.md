- [ ] 4 cups chicken broth
- [ ] 1 tsp salt
- [ ] 1 tsp garlic powder
- [ ] 1 tsp ground black pepper
- [ ] 2 tsp Italian seasoning
- [ ] 8 oz pasta (uncooked)
- [ ] 1 cup parmesan cheese
- [ ] 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
- [ ] 1 cup heavy whipping cream

## ðŸ¥˜ Instructions

### 1. Combine Ingredients
- Add the following to the crock pot:
    - Chicken breasts
    - Diced tomatoes
    - Pasta sauce
    - Chicken broth
    - Salt
    - Garlic powder
    - Black pepper
    - Italian seasoning

### 2. Slow Cook
- Cover crock pot with lid
- Cook on LOW for 6-8 hours or HIGH for 3-4 hours

### 3. Shred Chicken
- Once cooked, remove the chicken from the crockpot
- Shred the chicken and set aside

### 4. Cook Pasta
- Add the uncooked pasta to the crockpot
- Cover and let it cook for about 30 minutes

### 5. Finish
- Return shredded chicken to the pot
- Stir in the mozzarella cheese, shredded parmesan cheese, and heavy whipping cream
- Stir until cheeses are melted and combined
- Serve and enjoy!

## ðŸ’¡ Notes / Tips
- This is a "dump and go" slow cooker meal, perfect for busy days.
- Any short pasta shape (penne, rotini, shells) works well.
