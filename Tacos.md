- [ ] 1 lime, juiced
- [ ] 1 tsp olive oil

## ðŸ¥˜ Instructions

### 1. Sear ground beef
- Heat a cast iron pan over medium heat
- Add olive oil to hot pan and coat the entire surface
- Add ground beef and press into pan
- Cook undisturbed for several minutes until browned on one side
- Flip and brown the other side

### 2. Add dry ingredients
- Add all the seasonings to the pan
- Break up ground beef while mixing ingredients
- Continue cooking until fully browned

### 3. Deglaze the pan
- Add water and lime juice to pan
- Scrape browned bits from the bottom of the pan
- Cook until liquid is almost all evaporated
- Remove from heat

### 4. Serve
- Serve with warmed tortillas & chihuahua cheese
