### 1. Prepare Egg Mixture
- Beat eggs in a large bowl
- Add milk, dry mustard, and salt
- Whisk to combine and set aside

### 2. Cook Sausage
- Cook pork sausage in a skillet until browned and cooked through
- Drain excess fat

### 3. Assemble
- Grease a 13x9 inch Pyrex pan
- Layer the cubed bread, cooked sausage, and shredded cheddar cheese in the pan
- Pour the egg mixture evenly over the top of the layered ingredients

### 4. Chill
- Cover the pan
- Refrigerate overnight to allow the bread to absorb the liquid
