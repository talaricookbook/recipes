## ðŸ›’ Ingredients

### Dough
- [ ] 2 (10 ounce) cans refrigerated crescent roll dough

### Filling
- [ ] 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, diced
- [ ] 3/4 cup white sugar
- [ ] 1 1/2 teaspoons lemon juice
- [ ] 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- [ ] 2 teaspoons sour cream

### Icing
- [ ] 1 cup confectioners sugar
- [ ] 1 tablespoon milk
- [ ] 1 tablespoon butter, softened

## ðŸ¥˜ Instructions

### 1. Prepare Oven & Pan
- Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
- Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking pan

### 2. Base Layer
- Line bottom of baking pan with 1 can of crescent rolls
- Pinch all seams together to seal

### 3. Make Filling
- In a large bowl, mix together cream cheese, white sugar, lemon juice, vanilla extract, and sour cream
- Spread Filling evenly on top of the crescent roll base

### 4. Top Layer
- Place second can of rolls on top of the filling
- (Optional: You may want to gently pinch seams of top layer as well, though not strictly specified, it's good practice)

### 5. Bake
- Bake in preheated oven for 20-30 minutes, until golden brown

### 6. Make Icing
- In a small bowl, stir together confectioners sugar, milk, and butter until smooth

### 7. Finish
- After danish has cooled slightly (or completely), drizzle with icing before serving

## ðŸ’¡ Notes / Tips
- Ensure the cream cheese is softened or diced small for easier mixing.
- Let it cool sufficiently so the icing doesn't melt completely away.
- Use a stand mixer to make the filling
